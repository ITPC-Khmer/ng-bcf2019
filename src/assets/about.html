About Company

  

COOOZONE (CAMBODIA) ENTREPRISE has been established since 2013 by the firstly new Cambodian innovation of web designing & developing on Software and Application, storing the Global Company & Institution Information Directory for serving the multi-national visitors or clients around the world.


COOOZONE (CAMBODIA) ENTREPRISE which is officially registered under Ministry of Commerce, and registered its Global Trade Mark under the Worldwide Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) for its International Brand "COOOZONE" as a Leading International Provider of Information-Directory Service, is legally announced and operated the commercial launch in Asia Continent.

Today, COOOZONE (CAMBODIA) ENTREPRISE hardly work and go directly to meet the Businessmen, Investors, NGO’s, and Governmental Ministries & Authorities, to request and accept their Name Cards, listing into our Company Website naming as www.cooozone.com, in order to make them easy in communicating each other for the Business Relation & Official Relationship Purpose.

Our Company Website, www.cooozone.com, has been arranged & ordered All Companies, NGOs’ and Governmental Ministries & Authorities’ information in our 30 Business Categories, Business Names, and Business Types. This website has been developed and innovated up to provide the Global Information to multi-national Visitors & Clients to serve their Business & Relation purpose.

Moreover, this website also has been focused on the Product (Goods) & Services Display for their both profit and non-profit aims, and help them to make the long-term business & relation around the world effectively & efficiently. Anyway, our Company Managements have designed and produced the Business Partner Card & Family Member Card (EHM Cards & EHP Cards), to distribute in public to all multi-national members in order to promptly connect their product/service supply to their customers’ demand, and to supplement/ satisfy each other for their long-term Benefit or Non-benefit purpose so well.

COMPANY VISION

  

To become a Leader of Business Directory, Partnership & Membership with a strong global identity and a loyal visitors’, customers’ and business partners’ supports.

To benefit the world with the information directory & marketing network through developing and maintaining the business partners and EHM card & EHP card members.
